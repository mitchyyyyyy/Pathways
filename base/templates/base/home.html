Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know About Our Credit Solutions

Find answers to common questions about our loan products, application process, and financial services in Kenya.

What are the loan requirements?

Requirements include valid ID, proof of income (payslips or business statements), bank statements for the last 6 months, and KRA PIN. Additional requirements may apply based on loan type and amount.

What are your interest rates?

Our interest rates start from 1.5% monthly, varying based on loan type, amount, and term. We offer competitive rates compared to other financial institutions in Kenya, with transparent fee structures.

How long does loan processing take?

Most loans are processed within 24 hours after submitting complete documentation. Emergency loans can be processed in as little as 3 hours for existing clients with good credit history.

What are the repayment terms?

We offer flexible repayment terms ranging from 1 to 48 months. Payments can be made through M-PESA, bank transfer, or direct debit. Early repayment is allowed with no penalties.
